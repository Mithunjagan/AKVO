The Science

From Air to Aqua

We mimic the Earth's natural hydrological cycle within a compact, hyper-efficient system.

01

Air Intake

Ambient air is drawn through an antimicrobial air filtration matrix, capturing macroscopic dust and pollen.

02

Condensation

Air passes over coils chilled below the dew point. Vapor compression forces a phase transition from gas to liquid.

03

Purification

Water flows through sediment filters, activated carbon, UV sterilization, and Ozone treatment to neutralize pathogens.

04

Mineralization

We reintroduce essential Calcium and Magnesium, balancing the pH to ~7.6 for fresh, alkaline drinking water.
